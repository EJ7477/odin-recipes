Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Craving the perfect homemade cookie? This classic chocolate chip cookie recipe delivers everything you want—crispy golden edges, soft and chewy centers, and rich, melty chocolate in every bite. It is simple enough for beginners but reliable enough to become your go-to favorite, whether you’re baking for a cozy night in or sharing with friends. One batch, and your kitchen will smell like pure comfort—you might never go back to store-bought again.

Ingredients

  • Butter: 1 Cup
  • White Sugar: 1 Cup
  • Brown Sugar: 1 cup
  • Eggs: 2
  • Vanilla: 2 teaspoons
  • Baking Soda: 1 teaspoon
  • Hot Water: 2 teaspoons
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Flour: 3 cups
  • Chocolate Chips: 2 Cups
  • Walnuts (optional): 1 Cup

Steps

  1. Gather ingrediants
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  3. Beat butter and sugars together in large bowl
  4. Beat and stir in eggs and vanilla
  5. Add baking soda and salt and mix
  6. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts
  7. Spoon out balls of dough onto ungreased baking sheets 2 inches apart
  8. Bake for 10 minutes
  9. Cool on baking sheets then transfer to wire rack
  10. Serve and enjoy!
