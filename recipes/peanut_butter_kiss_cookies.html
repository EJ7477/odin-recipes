Peanut Butter Kiss Cookies

Description

Sweet, simple, and irresistibly nostalgic, these peanut butter kiss cookies are the perfect blend of soft, chewy peanut butter goodness and a rich chocolate center. With just a handful of ingredients, they come together quickly, making them ideal for last-minute baking or holiday treats. Each bite delivers that classic sweet-and-salty combo, topped with a melty chocolate kiss pressed right into the center—creating a cookie that’s as fun to make as it is to eat. One batch, and you’ll understand why this timeless favorite disappears almost instantly.

Ingredients

  • White Sugar: 1 Cup
  • Peanut Butter: 1 Cupp
  • Eggs: 1 Large Egg
  • Hershey Kisses: 18 Kisses

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix together sugar, peanut butter, and egg in large bowl
  3. Shape dough into small, one inch balls
  4. Place dough balls on ungreased cookie sheet
  5. Bake for 10 minutes
  6. Take out cookies and place hershey kiss in center of each one
  7. Freeze in air tight container or serve and enjoy!
