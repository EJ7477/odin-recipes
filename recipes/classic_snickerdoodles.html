Classic Snickerdoodles

Description

Soft, cozy, and full of warm cinnamon flavor, these classic snickerdoodles are the kind of cookie that never goes out of style. Each bite is perfectly tender with a slight tang and rolled in a sweet cinnamon-sugar coating that creates that signature crackly finish. Made with simple pantry ingredients like flour, sugar, and cream of tartar, they come together easily and bake into soft, chewy cookies with just the right balance of sweetness and spice. Whether you’re baking for a crowd or just craving something comforting, this timeless favorite is always a winning choice.

Ingredients

  • Shortening: 1 Cup
  • White Sugar: 1 1/2 Cups
  • Large Eggs: 2
  • All Purpose Flour (sifted): 2 3/4 Cups
  • Baking Soda: 1 Teaspoon
  • Cream of Tartar: 2 Teaspoons
  • Salt: 1/2 Teaspoon
  • White Sugar: 2 Tablespoons
  • Gound Cinnamon: 2 Teaspoons

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cream together shortening and sugar
  3. Mix in eggs one at a time
  4. Mix together flour, baking soda, cream of tarter, and salt in seperate bowl
  5. Add dry ingredients to creamed mixture
  6. In a small shallow bowl, stir together the extra sugar and cinnamon
  7. Roll the dough into walnut sized balls and roll them in sugar mixture
  8. Place cookies on greased cookie sheet 2 inches from each other
  9. Bake 8 to 10 minutes
  10. Serve and enjoy!
